Submissions

Thank you for your interest in submitting to Aporia. Our next call for submissions will take place early 2027.

POETRY & PROSE

  • We accept submissions from emerging and established Canadian writers between the ages of 15-30.
  • Please send us a short third-person bio under 50 words and indicate in the form if you wish to go by a pseudonym.
  • We accept poetry up to 50 lines, and prose up to 1000 words. When submitting, please include a line or word count for poetry or prose, respectively.
  • Each writer may submit up to three poems or prose pieces.
  • All submissions should not contain identifying information in the file itself.

VISUAL

  • Minimum 300 DPI quality.
  • Each artist may submit up to three pieces.
  • All submissions should not contain identifying information in the file itself.

OTHER INFORMATION

  • An honorarium of $20 per piece will be given to selected writers through e-transfer.
  • All submitters will be contacted within 2-3 months of the application deadline.
  • We encourage simultaneous submissions, but please let us know if your piece is accepted somewhere else.
